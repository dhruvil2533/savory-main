Peanut Butter Bliss Balls

ingredients:

  • 14 medjool dates, pitted
  • 200g raw or roasted almonds
  • 4 tbsp savory peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp cacao powder
  • 1-2 tbsp water, if needed
  • Prep Time: 5 mins.
  • Cook Time: 5 mins.
  • Total Time: 10 mins.
  • Yield: 10-12 pancakes

Method

  1. Line a baking tray with baking paper
  2. Place all of the ingredients into a food processor and blent until a dought begins to form
  3. Using your hands, roll tablespoonfuls of the mixture into snack-sized balls.
  4. Pour the crushed peanuts onto a plate or flat surface and roll the balls in the nuts to coat.
  5. Return them to the prepared tray and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
