Peanut Butter Apple Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon Creamy Peanut Butter
  • 1 slice crusty white bread
  • 1/4 medium apple, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon golden raisins
  • Ground cinnamon

Method

  1. Spread peanut butter over bread. Top with apple and raisins.
  2. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
  3. Enjoy your Sandwich
